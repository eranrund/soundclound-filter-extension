Privacy Policy — SoundCloud Feed Filter

Last updated: March 2026

What this extension does

SoundCloud Feed Filter is a Chrome extension that hides short tracks from your SoundCloud feed based on a minimum duration you set.

Data collection

This extension collects no data. It does not track you, profile you, or transmit anything to any server.

What is stored locally

Your preferences are saved in your browser using chrome.storage.local:

  • Minimum duration threshold
  • Filter mode (collapse or remove)
  • Whether to filter playlists
  • Widget position on screen

This data never leaves your device.

Third parties

No data is shared with any third party. The extension has no analytics, no crash reporting, and no external dependencies loaded at runtime.
